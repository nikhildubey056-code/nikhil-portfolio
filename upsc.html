Why Sociology?
🏘️
Understanding India
Sociology helps decode caste, class, gender, and religion in India — the very forces a civil servant must navigate and address every single day in the field.
⚖️
Policy Insight
From Durkheim to Ambedkar — sociological theories illuminate why policies succeed or fail, and how institutions shape human behavior across generations.
🔍
Critical Thinking
Studying social structures, stratification, and change trains the mind to ask harder questions: Who benefits? Who is excluded? What are the unintended consequences?
💡
The CS Advantage
My MCA background gives me a unique edge — I can apply computational thinking to social problems, bridging data analysis with humanistic understanding of ground realities.
